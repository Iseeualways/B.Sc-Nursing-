B.Sc Nursing Entrance Exam Syllabus & Eligibility

Take a look at some important details, such as the Karnataka B.Sc Nursing syllabus for entrance exam 2025 and the B.Sc Nursing entrance exam eligibility.

Karnataka B.Sc Nursing Entrance Exam Eligibility

  • Candidates must have passed Physics, Chemistry, Biology and English papers individually in 2nd PUC/ 10+2 and must have secured a minimum of 45% aggregate marks in PCB (40% for OBC/ SC/ ST).

Karnataka B.Sc Nursing Syllabus for Entrance Exam 2026

  • All topics covered in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology in PUC/10+2

KCET 2026 Exam Tips

Your dream of becoming a healthcare professional starts here. Navigating the KCET 2026 Nursing Process can feel overwhelming, but you don't have to do it alone. We've compiled the ultimate guide to help you manage everything from your initial application to the final round of counselling. Follow these expert tips to ensure your path to nursing is smooth and stress-free.

Complete your KCET application process well in advance to avoid errors and confusion.

Ensure your exam preparation is in line with the syllabus. Familiarize yourself with the complete syllabus.

Practice previous year questions and mock tests at least 10 times before the exam.

Properly plan how to reach the exam centre in advance. Better to be 3 hours early than one minute late.

Carry all your important documents, like your admit card, along with other essentials.

Check the provisional answer key after the exam and estimate your score.

Apply for counselling and select 20-30 top colleges to maximize your chances of securing a seat.
